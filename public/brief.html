The real-time difference

A taxi leaves you guessing. An Uber shows you the ride.

Hail a taxi and you stand at the kerb, hoping. Book an Uber and you watch the car approach, see the driver, and know the arrival time. InSight does the same for compliance: every duty holder's status, live, instead of finding out after it has gone wrong.

Without it

Compliance is scattered across spreadsheets, email threads and other people's document systems. You assume each duty holder is doing their part, and only find the gap once it has already become a problem on site.

With InSight

Every duty holder's position updates the moment they record it. You see signed off, awaiting review, and anything slipping behind, live. You act on information, not assumptions.
